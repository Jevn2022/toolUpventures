# Color Palette Usage Guide

This palette is inspired by extreme exploration environments: ice, water, rock, forest, and human presence in high-risk, high-control settings. It is designed to balance calm, depth, and trust with moments of energy and action.

---

## Palette Overview

**Core Colors** form the backbone of the system. They should dominate layouts and define the brand atmosphere.

- **Pale Sky (#bcd4dc)**  
  Light, open, and breathable. Use for primary backgrounds, large surfaces, and to create visual rest.

- **Tropical Teal (#32aeae)**  
  The primary brand color. Represents depth, exploration, and mastery. Ideal for hero sections, primary UI elements, and key brand surfaces.

- **Graphite (#2e2f30)**  
  Structural neutral. Use for typography, icons, dividers, and UI scaffolding. Provides seriousness and contrast.

---

## Accent Colors

Accent colors represent human energy, movement, and decision making. Use sparingly.

- **Autumn Leaf (#ff5900ff)**  
  High-energy accent. Best for CTAs, highlights, focus states, and moments where action is required.

- **Amber Gold (#f2b705)**  
  Signal and alert color. Use for warnings, progress indicators, emphasis, or positive feedback states.

Recommended usage: accents should not exceed 10 percent of any single layout.

---

## Support Colors

Support colors add warmth, natural balance, and editorial richness.

- **Hunter Green (#3f5f4b)**  
  Evokes resilience and nature. Suitable for secondary sections, sustainability messaging, or calm UI states.

- **Khaki Beige (#c6b08a)**  
  Warm neutral. Ideal for cards, content backgrounds, and long-form layouts where pure white feels too stark.

---

## Neutrals

- **Bright Snow (#f7f9fa)**  
  Clean white alternative. Use for text-heavy areas, UI backgrounds, and contrast against dark elements.

- **Ink Black (#0f1e2a)**  
  Deep, atmospheric dark tone. Preferred over pure black for dark mode, footers, and immersive sections.

---

## Recommended Ratios

- 60 percent core colors (Pale Sky, Tropical Teal, Graphite)
- 30 percent neutrals and support tones
- 10 percent or less accent colors

This ensures a premium, controlled aesthetic rather than a chaotic or overly aggressive look.

---

## Practical Applications

- **Branding**: Tropical Teal as primary brand color, Graphite for typography, Autumn Leaf for CTAs.
- **UI Design**: Pale Sky or Bright Snow backgrounds, Ink Black for dark mode, Amber Gold for system feedback.
- **Editorial and Decks**: Khaki Beige and Hunter Green to soften layouts and add environmental context.

---

## Accessibility Notes

- Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background, especially when using Pale Sky or Khaki Beige.
- Accent colors should not be the sole indicator of state or action.

---

This palette is designed to scale across digital products, brand systems, and editorial use while maintaining a consistent narrative of exploration, trust, and human performance in extreme environments.
